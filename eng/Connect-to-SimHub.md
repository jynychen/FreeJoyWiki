## Connecting FreeJoy to SimHub:

In SimHub, on the left, select "Devices," click "Import device from file," and select the [FreeJoy.shdevice](../3rd-party/FreeJoy.shdevice) file. In the "USB PID/VID configuration" tab, configure the VID/PID as in the FreeJoy configurator and the number of LEDs.
For instructions on configuring SimHub, see its [wiki](https://github.com/SHWotever/SimHub/wiki/LED-Editor-guide)
